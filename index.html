WHO WE ARE

We bridge the gap between high school and college, and make learning accessible and fun (no, really) for both high schoolers and college students.

The transition between high school and college can also be especially difficult, and our goal is to make that as smooth and fun as possible! What better way is there to learn integration by unrolling a roll of paper towels? Or just getting out of sticky situations in physics with some marshmallow fluff to help you understand viscosity?

Our ultimate goal is to make learning fun and encourage curiosity and innovation, so it’s definitely something we’ll be doing on our own adventure as well!
